About YouthHire

Why Choose YouthHire?

Founded to empower young talent, YouthHire specializes in helping students under 18 find meaningful part-time work and internships that align with school schedules. We focus on entry-level opportunities that build skills and confidence for future careers.

  • Part-time and flexible schedules
  • Mentor support and skill building
  • Safe, legal opportunities for under 18
  • Parents/caregivers involved in process

Opportunities for Young Talents

Web Development Internships

Learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript basics while building real websites for local businesses and community projects.

Mobile App Creation

Design and develop simple mobile apps using beginner-friendly tools and frameworks, perfect for teens with coding interest.

KYC Data Assistant

Entry-level data entry roles in compliance verification, helping with document organization and basic research tasks.

Game Testing

Play pre-release games and provide feedback on gameplay, bugs, and user experience in a fun, part-time role.

Graphic Design Assistant

Create social media graphics, posters, and digital art using simple design tools while learning professional techniques.

Content Creation

Write blog posts, create videos, and manage social media for teen-focused brands and educational content.
